Teaching Girls in Zimbabwe how to Code

Weekly after school clubs at local community centers to improve computer literacy among teenage girls. Short workshops to get Zimbabwean girls excited about the limitless world of computer programming

Members from Diverse Backgrounds

Our members represent the rich diversity present within our borders and beyond. Girls come from varying economic, social and cultural backgrounds. Zimbo Py Clubs are therefore found in variety of locations.

Connecting Our Girls to Women who Inspire Them

We are creating a sisterhood to encourage more girls to see themselves entering the world of technology. Meeting or hearing about women who are doing well in tech fields will inspire them to know they can do the same! Sign up to become a mentor or share your story.
